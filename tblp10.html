Web Marketing Dashboard

Introduction

This dashboard provides a holistic view of essential performance indicators and user engagement metrics for an online platform. Join me as I unveil the key highlights and insights gleaned from this endeavor, shedding light on the pivotal role of data-driven analysis in shaping successful web marketing strategies.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • Analyzing and presenting crucial KPIs such as Total Exits (139,310), Average Page Load Time (72 seconds), and more provided invaluable insights into the platform's performance.

Device Overview (2018-2019)

  • Developing a dynamic dashboard enabled us to delve into user interactions across desktops, mobile devices, and tablets, offering rich insights into user behavior on different platforms.

Top 5 Unique Pageviews by Page Title

  • Identifying the top 5 unique pageviews based on page titles allowed for actionable insights, guiding content optimization and user engagement strategies.

Top 5 Unique Pageviews by Country

  • Conducting a global analysis to determine the top 5 countries generating the most unique pageviews empowered targeted marketing efforts and expanded the platform's international reach.

Concluding Insights

Extracting actionable insights, such as the importance of optimizing content based on user preferences and enhancing website load times, underscored the significance of data-driven decision-making in web marketing and user experience optimization.
